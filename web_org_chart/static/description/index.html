Organization Chart

Organization charts are effective, transparent and they communicate a lot about an organization in just a single glance. This module is compatible to Odoo 10 Community and Enterprise Editions.

"an org chart makes it easier for people to understand large amounts of information as a visual picture rather than as a table of names and numbers."

An organization chart is a graphical depiction of the roles and structure within an organization. The charts also diagram the relationship between those roles and they are a simple way to visualize how work flows within a business.
